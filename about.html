About Us

I'm Josh Boothman - a freelance web developer based in Mole Creek, Tasmania.

I build websites for small businesses and not-for-profits

with a focus on clear communication, authentic design, and local connection.

Small Business Know-How

My wife and I have been small business owners in the area of technology for most of our lives, recently running a hospitality business here in Mole Creek for six years. In that time, we built strong relationships with locals and learned firsthand the challenges small businesses face - especially when it comes to getting online.

Meander Valley Web Design was born from that experience. It's our way of helping other community-focused businesses show up online with confidence with websites that are easy to use, reflect who they are, and grow with them over time.

What I Value

  • Transparency: You'll always know what you're getting, what it costs, and how it works.
  • Relationship: I work with you like a partner, not just a provider.
  • Careful Craft: Every detail matters - from colours to copy.
  • Local Insight: I live and work in Meander Valley. I know the pace, the vibe, and what makes our community tick.

Whether you're just getting started or looking to refresh an old site, I'd love to help.
